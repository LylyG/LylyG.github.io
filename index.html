About Me

I want to actualize the client’s vision in the greatest way possible. As a creative kid that loved art and reading, I started my career in copywriting and later graphic design but something was missing. I knew that anything I created would have to migrate to the internet to be seen and heard.

When Spoonflower offered a scholarship to attend The Iron Yard, I leapt at the opportunity. Now I'm searching for a career that will continue to let me tinker with ideas and build elegant solutions for clients.

Latest Projects

SpoonBoards

Enhancing the customer's experience using Spoonflower's API

SpoonBoards is a Ruby on Rails app that allows the user to curate themed collections of designs and purchase fabric of those designs to generate more conversions. Users have the option to log in using Pinterest, Facebook or they can register new accounts. Then the user creates a board, browses through thousands of Spoonflower designs and then add them to the board.
